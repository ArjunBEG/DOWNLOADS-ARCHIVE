About this tool

This tool uses regexpu to transpile ES6 Unicode regular expressions (with a u flag) to equivalent ES5.

The Unicode property escapes functionality is based on a proposal and may or may not reflect what eventually gets specified. The same applies for the s (dotAll) flag.
