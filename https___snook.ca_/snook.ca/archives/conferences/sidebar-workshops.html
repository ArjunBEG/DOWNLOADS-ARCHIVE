Sidebar Workshops

The Sidebar Creative crew are putting together a workshop! Come join us in Washington D.C. on November 12th as we step through the front-end development of an example project. We'll go from the design process through to implementation with plenty of take-home tricks along the way.

This is an intermediate-level workshop targeted to those who already have some experience doing front-end development and are looking to sharpen the saw or expand their skills in specific areas, be they design, slicing, HTML, CSS or JavaScript. We'll be touching on a variety of tools including Adobe Photoshop and Fireworks.

The original early-bird pricing was today and while we've Twittered about the upcoming workshop, we didn't have a chance to blog about it until now. So, to be fair, we're extending the early-bird price of $499 until this Friday (the date on the site will be updated shortly).

Speaking of Twitter, be sure to follow @sidebarworkshop for ongoing details of this and future workshops.

This is a small workshop with only 30 spots — well, less than that now since we've already sold a bunch! Be sure to get your seat now before they're all gone. We hope to see you there!

Other Locations

Probably the most common request we get is, "come to my town!" We hear you! We're already finalizing plans for a San Diego workshop in January and we'll be posting the details soon. If you'd like us to come to your town, feel free to let us know.
