Speaking at FOWD

Only four more weeks until the Future of Web Design conference hits New York City. I'll be there speaking on Ajax and Design. I promise not to be all technical... instead, I plan to talk about best practices when it comes to using Ajax to solve design problems. The conference is gearing up to be a lot of fun. While the workshops appear to be all sold out, conference tickets should still be available and, at a paltry $195, it's a steal.

The folks behind FOWD asked me a few questions and I graciously answered. Be sure to check out the other speaker spotlights that have been posted.

The conference is on November 7th and 8th which is a Wednesday/Thursday but I'll be sticking around for Friday, as well. If you'd like to get together on the 9th, let me know.

Hope to see you there!
